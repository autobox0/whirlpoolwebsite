About the Whirlpool Galaxy

The Whirlpool Galaxy (M51) is a grand-design spiral galaxy located in the constellation Canes Venatici. Famous for its dramatic spiral arms and interacting companion galaxy, it’s the crown jewel of deep-space tourism.

Highlights include:

  • Over 100 billion sparkling stars
  • Active galactic nucleus experience
  • Companion galaxy sightseeing tours
  • Zero-gravity pool loungers
