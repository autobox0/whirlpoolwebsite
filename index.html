WHIRLPOOL GALAXY

Welcome to the Cosmos

The Whirlpool Galaxy (M51) is a stunning grand-design spiral galaxy located approximately 23 million light-years away in the constellation Canes Venatici. Its distinctive spiral structure showcases beautiful, graceful arms that rotate majestically through space.

This magnificent galaxy spans about 76,000 light-years across and is home to hundreds of billions of stars. The Whirlpool is particularly famous for its perfect spiral shape and active star formation regions within its rotating arms.

Discovered in 1773, the Whirlpool Galaxy remains one of the most photographed and studied galaxies, captivating astronomers and space enthusiasts alike with its cosmic beauty and dynamic stellar nurseries.
